Leverage is how a single developer reaches beyond the limits of their own hands.
Skills, tools, agents, and codified expertise extend what one person can deliver —
without diluting what they design.

The distinction that matters is between amplification and abdication. The same tool —
an agent, a generator, a harness — can extend an engaged mind or replace an absent
one, depending on whether the developer stays in the loop. Leverage is only leverage
when judgment is still driving.
